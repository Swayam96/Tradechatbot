About {{ app_name }}

{{ app_name }} is an educational trade and finance chatbot powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). It answers your questions using content crawled from trusted web sources — not from static PDFs or books.

How it works

  1. We crawl and extract article content from the configured knowledge base website.
  2. Content is cleaned, chunked, and converted into vector embeddings.
  3. When you ask a question, relevant passages are retrieved and sent to an LLM.
  4. The model generates an answer grounded in those sources, with citations.
